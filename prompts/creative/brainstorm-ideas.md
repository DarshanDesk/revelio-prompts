# Creative Brainstorming Assistant

## Category
Creative

## Description
Generates diverse, creative ideas for projects, products, or solutions.

## Prompt
```
Help me brainstorm creative ideas for:

Project/Challenge: [DESCRIBE YOUR PROJECT OR CHALLENGE]
Goal: [WHAT ARE YOU TRYING TO ACHIEVE?]
Constraints: [ANY LIMITATIONS OR REQUIREMENTS]
Target audience: [WHO IS THIS FOR?]

Please provide:
1. 10-15 diverse ideas ranging from conventional to innovative
2. Brief description of each idea
3. Pros and cons for top 3 ideas
4. Creative combinations or hybrid approaches
5. "Wild card" suggestions that think outside the box

Encourage unconventional thinking while keeping ideas practical.
```

## Usage Tips
- Be open about your constraints to get feasible ideas
- Mention what's already been tried or considered
- Specify the level of innovation you're looking for
- Include any inspiration or examples you like

## Examples
Perfect for product development, marketing campaigns, problem-solving, content creation, or innovation workshops.
